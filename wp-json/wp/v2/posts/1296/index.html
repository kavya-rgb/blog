Background: I have 8ish years off and on of graphic design experience and about a year and some change of limited web dev experience.<\/p>\n

I think I’m being underpaid for the amount of work I’m doing. I started my current company in January with the title of Front End Developer. I’m the only full time dev at the company and we outsource all of our back end development another company. When interviewing I was had the understanding that I would be given mockups and build pages and stuff based on those but I have a feeling I’m being taken advantage of since I have a background in graphic design. My responsibilities now are front end dev, web design, graphic design and what feel like “lead” front end developer since as of Monday I’m being told I have to check the site regularly to make sure nothing is broken.<\/p>\n

Wen interviewing originally with the recruiter I was told I’d be at 75k with a possibility of being a creative director down the road but then in my in person interview with the ceo I was told 80k but then after I did my test I got an offer for 64k.<\/p>\n

My 3 month review is in 2ish weeks and I’m not sure how to approach any of this. On one hand I would like to ask for a raise or to be considered for creative director since I’m covering so many things but I’m also kinda nervous because of my imposter syndrome since I’m working with a language I’m still pretty new at. I drive about three hours \/ 120 miles a day a day round trip for this job and with gas prices going up its getting a little expensive.<\/p>\n

I’m not sure what to do. I can’t find another company since my last job and this one are both less than a year it looks like I job hop alot. I like the company and everyone I work with for the most part but I just feel like I’m being taken advantage of a little bit. Any advice is welcome!<\/p>\n\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
