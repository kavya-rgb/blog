Margin collapse is an interesting concept in CSS margins that you should know, understand and be conscious of. It is the process where you apply a margin of 10px on top, the bottom of an element, apply another 10px on top, the bottom of the next element, and end up having a 10px space between the two-element instead of 20px.<\/p>\n
