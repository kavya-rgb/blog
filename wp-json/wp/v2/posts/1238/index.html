Due to a growing influx of questions on this topic, it has been decided to commit a monthly thread dedicated to this topic to reduce the number of repeat posts on this topic. These types of posts will no longer be allowed in the main thread.<\/p>\n

Many of these questions are also addressed in the sub FAQ or may have been asked in [previous monthly career threads](https:\/\/www.reddit.com\/r\/webdev\/search?q=flair%3AMonthlyCareerThread&restrict_sr=on&sort=new&t=all).<\/p>\n

Subs dedicated to these types of questions include [r\/cscareerquestions](https:\/\/www.reddit.com\/r\/cscareerquestions\/)\/ for general and opened ended career questions and [r\/learnprogramming](https:\/\/www.reddit.com\/r\/learnprogramming\/)\/ for early learning questions.<\/p>\n

A general recommendation of topics to learn to become industry ready include:<\/p>\n

[HTML\/CSS\/JS Bootcamp](https:\/\/www.udemy.com\/course\/javascript-beginners-complete-tutorial)<\/p>\n

[Version control](https:\/\/www.atlassian.com\/git\/tutorials\/what-is-version-control)<\/p>\n

[Automation](https:\/\/blog.logrocket.com\/tools-and-modern-workflow-for-front-end-developers-505c7227e917\/)<\/p>\n

[Front End Frameworks (React\/Vue\/Etc)](https:\/\/www.freecodecamp.org\/news\/complete-guide-for-front-end-developers-javascript-frameworks-2019\/)<\/p>\n

[APIs and CRUD](https:\/\/www.freecodecamp.org\/news\/crud-operations-using-vanilla-javascript-cd6ee2feff67\/)<\/p>\n

[Testing (Unit and Integration)](https:\/\/raygun.com\/blog\/javascript-unit-testing-frameworks\/)<\/p>\n

[Common Design Patterns](https:\/\/addyosmani.com\/resources\/essentialjsdesignpatterns\/book\/) (free ebook)<\/p>\n

You will also need a portfolio of work with 4-5 personal projects you built, and a resume\/CV to apply for work.<\/p>\n
