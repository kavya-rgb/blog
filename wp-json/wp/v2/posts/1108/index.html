Hi Everyone \ud83d\udc4b,<\/p>\n

This is my second time writing for #shecoded.<\/p>\n

Last year, I found my community and no longer be alone<\/a>.
And since then, so many things happened and I learned so much more throughout the year.<\/p>\n

\n
\n <\/a>
\n Challenges That I Have Overcome
\n<\/h2>\n

I had a massive burnout<\/a>, followed by some imposter syndromes.
I thought I would give up, completely.
But after taking some break (and with some support from my communities) I got back and even made some more achievements \u2764.<\/p>\n

\n
\n <\/a>
\n My Achievements in 2021
\n<\/h2>\n

\n
\n <\/a>
\n Collaboration & Open Source
\n<\/h2>\n

As I mentioned in the title, I learned much more throughout 2021.<\/p>\n

I was very happy that I got accepted for a React project collaboration program at The Collab Lab<\/a>!
I learned about how to collaborate on a project in a remote team and how to use Git & GitHub.
I still need to learn more about React. But I definitely learned so much about teamwork. A valuable experience that I can’t get as a self-taught developer.<\/p>\n

As I got accepted at The Collab Lab, the HacktoberFest 2021 happened.
With the collaboration going on, I would be very happy if I could at least contribute to one open-source.
But in the end, I completed the HacktoberFest with 8 contributions in total.<\/p>\n

This journey has grown my interest in open-source and accessibility tremendously.<\/p>\n

\n
\n <\/a>
\n Volunteering Journey
\n<\/h2>\n

I’m very grateful to take part as one of the Program Committee and posts’ tag moderator for CodeNewbie<\/a>‘s CodeLand 2021<\/a>.<\/p>\n

I’m also very proud and happy to be the Documentation Lead<\/strong> at Virtual Coffee<\/a>.<\/p>\n

And recently, I’m taking the offer to be one of the Code of Conduct mentors<\/strong> for our cohorts at The Collab Lab<\/a>!<\/p>\n

\n
\n <\/a>
\n My Goal
\n<\/h2>\n

I’m the type of person who doesn’t set a long-term goal.
I’m more of a go with a flow kind of person \ud83d\ude05.
But this year, I have a goal to push through with my learning and land my feet on my first job in tech! \ud83d\ude0a<\/p>\n
